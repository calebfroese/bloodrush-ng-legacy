About

The idea for bloodrush was first developed in 2013 and created as a private html5 game amonsgst friends and colleagues. After several months however, the server suffered a catastrophic hardware failure and much information and code was lost. It wasn't until several years later in 2017 when I had asked the creator of Bloodrush v1 for permission to recreate the concept. Work began on creating Bloodrush v2 from scratch in early January with a closed beta later on in the month.
