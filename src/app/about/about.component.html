About

Origin

The idea for bloodrush was first developed in 2013 and created as a private html5 game amonsgst friends and colleagues. After several months however, the server suffered a catastrophic hardware failure and much information and code was lost. It wasn't until several years\ later in 2017 when I had asked the creator of Bloodrush 1.0 for permission to recreate the concept. Work began on creating Bloodrush 2.0 from scratch in early January with a closed beta later on in the month.

Overview

Bloodrush is a non-demanding real time social team management game. The idea is that it is a game great for busy people who don't have time to sit and play MMOs, MOBAs, etc. In Bloodrush, you manage your team and players in a competitive league against friends. Players have attack, defence, and speed as main attributes which affect the gameplay on the field. It's up to the teams to find the best strategy in terms of player placement. These games can however result in serious player injuries and even deaths, so don't get too attached to these poorly drawn figures!
A big part of bloodrush is the customizability of your team. You can create and use a custom team logo, team uniform, and even an anthem to play after a game victory. Leagues can be created after a certain amount of team experience, and users may create their own private or public leagues for others to join. Games are generated daily, in the timezone of the league owner in a round robin fashion.
